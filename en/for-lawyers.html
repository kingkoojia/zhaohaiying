For International Law Firms & Practitioners

Your trusted China-side partner for cross-border civil matters

Short version: If your client has assets in China — inheritance, property, bank deposits, or equity — and needs legal help on the China side, I handle the entire process. You refer, I execute, we keep your client informed every step of the way.

Cases I Handle

All matters involving Chinese assets where the client is overseas:

  • Inheritance of Chinese assets — Real estate, bank deposits, company shares, vehicles. Full-process handling including inheritance notarization, property registration, and asset transfer.
  • Property sale & fund repatriation — Assisting foreign nationals in selling Chinese real estate, completing tax clearance, foreign exchange purchase, and cross-border remittance.
  • Cross-border family matters — International divorce involving Chinese assets, child custody, recognition of foreign divorce judgments in China.
  • Estate planning for cross-border families — Dual-jurisdiction will planning, cross-border wealth succession structures.

Sample Case — Inheritance from Abroad

Situation: A US-based client inherited a Shanghai apartment from a deceased parent. Client could not travel to China due to work commitments.

What we did: Coordinated remotely — client sent a notarized and apostilled power of attorney from the US. We handled the inheritance notarization, property registration, and subsequent sale of the apartment. Total funds were remitted to the client's US bank account.

Timeline: 4 months from initial contact to funds received.
